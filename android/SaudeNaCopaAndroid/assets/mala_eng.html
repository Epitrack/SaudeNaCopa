International Agricultural Surveillance

  • Brazil has rules to control the entry of animals, plants and products in the country;
  • Before traveling, consult the Brazilian Embassy or Consulate about the requirements for the imports of animals, pants and other products;
  • Products which may not enter into Brazil without previous authorization and/or sanitary certification:
    • Fruits, vegetables, milk, cheese, butter, yogurt, dulce de leche, honey, wax, propolis, fresh or industrialized meats (ham, canned products, fish), fresh vegetables, food served on board;
    • Pets, insects, mollusks, bacteria and fungi, pet food, eggs, semen, embryos;
    • Pesticides and veterinary products (serum, vaccines, medications, among others), seedlings, seeds;
    • Wood and soil.
