Service Mobile Emergency (SAMU 192)

  • SAMU works 24 hours every day of the week, and is reached through telephone number 192. The call is free and can be performed by any landline or mobile phone site. SAMU performs emergency care and emergency via ambulance, anywhere: homes, workplaces and public roads. The team brings together doctors, nurses, auxiliary nurses and first responders who meet the urgencies of traumatic nature, clinical, pediatric, surgical, gynecological and mental health.
  • When you call the SAMU?
    • On the occurrence of cardio-respiratory problems;
    • In the cases of exogenous intoxication;
    • In the case of severe burns;
    • On the occurrence of maltreatment;
    • In the labors where there is risk of death of the mother or fetus;
    • In the cases of suicide attempts;
    • In the hypertensive crisis;
    • When There accidents / injuries to victims;
    • In the cases of drowning;
    • In the cases of electric shock;
    • In the accidents involving dangerous goods;
    • In the inter-hospital transfer of patients at risk of death.

Emergency care units (UPA 24h)

  • They provide care for patients affected by acute episodes of clinical nature and the first care of patients with trauma or surgical nature. Stabilize patients and perform a diagnostic investigation, by defining all cases, the necessity of routing the hospital services of greater complexity.
